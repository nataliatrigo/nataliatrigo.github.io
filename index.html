Natalia Trigo Tomasevich

My name is Natalia, and I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Operations and Technology Management at the UCLA Anderson School of Management. I am originally from Rancagua, Chile, and I hold a degree in Industrial Engineering and a Master’s degree in Operations Management, both from the University of Chile. My research interests include stochastic processes, multi-armed bandits, and operations research, with applications in artificial intelligence and healthcare.
